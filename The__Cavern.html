The Cavern

Back

The Cavern is an online muiltiplayer role playing game, made in Minecraft. It is currently still in development. The goal of this project is to make a large scale rpg experiance in Minecraft, incorperating various different aspects of past large servers and putting our own spins on them. It is not my project as the owner is CLWanserski, someone I have worked with on other minecraft servers. Myself and 6 others began working on it early in Augest of 2021.

Concepting into Planning

The original idea for this project was to create a large rpg in Minecraft incorperating aspects from prision servers, fantasy rpg servers, and more of the older popular servers we enjoyed. The owner of the project came to the starting two leads and pitched his idea and asked them to build a team, I was asked to join by one of them due to working with all of them before and my knoleage of Minecraft's system. Once the team was made we started planning the world first, we brainstormed things like what habitats we wanted, what tones would there be in those areas, and what kinds of civilistions would be there. We did start planning some of the general world story at this time but left much of it abstract till we could get in the enviroment and had a better idea of the mechanics we wanted.

Building and Testing

Due to the project being in Minecraft, most of the work is done in game. We made a team of builders, currently 7 strong, and got them to work building the different cities quickly. We were able to use a software called World Painter that allows you to make custom Minecraft worlds, we used this to make the world and than the builders build in that world. As for the back-end in the area I worked, the owner and I got to learning all we could of JavaScript, JSON, and Mojang's in-house Molang to be able to properly use their add-on support. We have at the moment added new entities, items, behaviors, interactable user interfaces, new textures for almost everything in the game. For the textures and ui designs we have an artist that is also or head of world design.

For testing all of this, for any of the back-end we can't just post it onto the realm all of us are working on as that creates down time for production and if it doesn't all work there will just be even more down time. So the owner and I test in solo worlds, compile it all up together by me sending the files through dropbox or me sending code snippets with directions on where they go in our discord server, test again, then either we bring the realm down to change the pack or we wait till the next Minecraft update since that will cause down time anyway and than the team will get a message when the owner brings it back up and whoever can gets on and tests that textures and the normal commands are working. Generally if someone worked on a certain thing in that update they also got on at somepoint to test it before going onto their next task, but our first prioity was getting the most used or seen asspects working so that the other teams can keep working.

Finishing

The Cavern is not done yet. I have also taken a step back from this project due to starting at Purdue and other projects needing more priority due to The Cavern having such a great team around it where my full dedication is not needed at the moment.
